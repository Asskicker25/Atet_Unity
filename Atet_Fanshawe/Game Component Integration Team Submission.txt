Controls :

Press Escape to Quit the Game.

Press A and D to Move Left and Right.

Press Space near corners to rotate the camera.

Press R to restart the game if you're dead. You can only restart if you're dead.

Press LeftControl near a pillar to push or pull the Mirror Pillar.


Your goal is the allign the light shine on the Chalice of Life using the Mirror Pillars
